All you Must Know About Language Lab

The English Language Laboratory is a very unique intitiative which primarily focuses on eradicating the fear of English from within the minds of students. Here, at the English lab, our aim is to enhance the Reading, Listening, Speaking and Writing skills of the young minds. The two or three hours in the lab is a relief for the students and at the same time the spoken English skills, which they will require at the time of placements, are polished.
